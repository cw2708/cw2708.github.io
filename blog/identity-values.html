In a previous job, I encountered a situation where a customer asked me to clarify information on a product that was mistaken by myself. I chose to be transparent and inform my manager about the error I had made informing the customer despite the potential fallout. Looking back, I believe I made the right choice and my manger appreciated my honesty and told me that it was better I had told him then not. Honesty is a core value for me, and compromising it would have eroded my integrity. While it was a difficult decision at the time, I’m proud that I upheld my principles.

Share an example of when you were trying to work productively with others, but there was resistance or tension. Discuss strategies you tried at the time, how effective they were, and your reflections on what other strategies you would try now and why.

In a team project at the UOA summer lab, I encountered resistance from a team member who was reluctant to collaborate and share workload. To address this, I tried fostering open communication by expressing understanding of their concerns and emphasising the shared goal. However, this approach had limited success due to entrenched attitudes. Reflecting on this, I realise the importance of early team alignment and setting clear expectations.
