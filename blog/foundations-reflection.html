What are the role of values, empathy, and self-awareness in learning and programming?

Guiding Principles: Values serve as guiding principles that shape how programmers approach their work. For example, integrity and honesty drive programmers to write clean, transparent, and ethical code. Decision Making: Programmers often encounter ethical dilemmas or situations where they must prioritize certain features or approaches. Their values influence these decisions, ensuring that they align with ethical standards and contribute positively to society. Team Collaboration: Shared values within a development team foster a cohesive work environment where members respect each other's contributions, communicate openly, and collaborate effectively toward common goals.
